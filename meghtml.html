Meet Meghan

Hi, my name is Meghan O’Malley and I’m currently living in Coronado, California. I’m a long time runner turned triathlete and have competed collegiately for the University of Colorado's triathlon team. I am a very dedicated and disciplined person who is willing to do all the little things in order to reach my goals. Additionally, I am very passionate about using information and data to improve myself, others, and the world around me. I was able to incorporate those passions into multiple projects while earning my bachelor’s degree in Information Science. There is a lot of overlap between human performance and data science. I am looking to use my skills and passions to aid the U.S. Military.
